Course Descriptions

Welcome! Whether you are just thinking about applying to Ryman Arts, or have been a student with us for some time, this is the place where you can find out what you need to know.

All students start with Foundation Drawing. Upon completion of each semester they reapply to move on to the next course level.

Foundation Drawing emphasizes the foundations of drawing technique and composition. The class includes an introduction to figure drawing.

Intermediate Drawing and Painting builds on the previous course, and introduces watercolor painting, more extensive use of color, figure drawing, and portraiture.

Advanced Art: Painting focuses on acrylic painting and mixed media in more extended projects. The class also includes figure study.
