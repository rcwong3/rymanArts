Keep in Touch

Ryman Arts alumni keep in touch through email, facebook, events, and the alumni association. They stay involved with Ryman Arts through positions such as teaching assistants, workshop artists, volunteers, and interns!
Please feel free to keep in touch or share your alumni stories by emailing alumni@ryman.org or connect with us on Facebook!

Featured Alumni

Cyan Vong

Cyan is an Art Director and Graphic Designer currently based in New York. She has been working in both Los Angeles and New York since obtaining her BFA in Advertising from Art Center College of Design in 2008, and most recently earning her MFA in Graphic Design at Yale University in 2014.

Adam Castellanos

To me the best part of the whole experience was being assigned two mentors that are my biggest inspirations, and the top talents in their field and they were Shiyoon Kim for Character Design and Bruce Smith for Animation!

Megan Roczey

When Ryman first announced the Marty Sklar Internship at Walt Disney Imagineering I knew it would be a great opportunity, but I did not know how I could fit into such an accomplished company. After a little bit of research I learned that Imagineering covered a wider variety of disciplines than I could have imagined. I started to think about why I loved going to Disneyland so much and what about the Disney Company I felt was so special.

Jesse Swanson

To present your work, and to then have that work critiqued by the incredible instructors and guest lecturers in the Ryman program was the sparking point for my career. This is how I learned to work with others, and bounce ideas and concepts with others in the field.

Stephan Nolan

I was extremely fortunate to have gotten this internship through Ryman. Because of it, I was able to see that there are more to jobs than punching in and out and staring at a screen in a dimly-lit cubicle. It’s cliché, but I’ll admit it – jobs can be fun, not only in terms of the work, but the environment itself. There’s something about working with people who love what they do that makes you love what you do even more.
